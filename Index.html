About me:

Hey all!

My family and I emigrated to the United States during 2008 to Arcadia, California in order to seek out the American Dream and get better education for my brother and I. Being a first-generation college student, I was able to scope out multiple scholarly subjects during my academic years spent at Arcadia High School and eventually at Pasadena City College. Years later, I was able to find my passion in Computer Science, Mathematics, and general Technology, which finally led me on the path to Majoring in Computer Science and Minoring in Mathematics. I’m a pretty experienced programmer with expertise in a ranging variety of software from Adobe Premiere Pro to Fruity Loops Studio with a specialization in programming softwares such as Clion, Visual Studio Code, and Codeblocks focusing on object-oriented programming languages such as C++, JAVA, and Python.

Since the pandemic started I decided to keep going to school full-time and learning different languages that my school didn’t need me to take on the side. I have learned a lot of data structures and algorithms implemented in object-oriented programming, but I have also been teaching myself programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript.

Before becoming a Computer Science major, I actually was very into film and arts and planned on majoring in that before I found my passion in Computer Science. Because of this, I was able to create a collection of short films, a photography collection, and learned a bunch of software such as Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and even taught myself to mix music on FL Studio and Ableton Live.

When I’m taking time for myself, I really like to spend my time playing video games, listening or making music, working out at the gym and especially playing sports (I used to be a soccer player!). I’m a huge Sony and Nintendo fan so this is where I got the idea of a 8-bit retro theme for this website. I hope you learned something about me and I look forward to meeting you all!
